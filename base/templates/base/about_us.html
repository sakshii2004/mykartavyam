ABOUT US

"Kartavyam" is a Sanskrit term that translates to "duty" or "responsibility" in English. It refers to the ethical and moral obligations that one is bound to fulfill in various aspects of life. In the broader Indian philosophical and spiritual context, kartavyam emphasizes the performance of one's duties.

Kartavyam is a platform designed to empower citizens and improve the quality of life in our communities by addressing everyday civic issues. Our mission is to provide a simple yet powerful way for people to report problems in their surroundings and bring the authorities' attention to their everyday issues. Whether it's potholes, streetlight failures, waste management, water supply disruptions, or any other public concern, Kartavyam allows users to easily log complaints and follow their progress. Our aim is to foster a more responsive and accountable system where every voice matters, and every issue can be addressed effectively.
